# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a premium, single-page solar energy marketing website for "SolarPeak Energy" with a dark glassmorphism theme, 3D visuals, interactive sections, and full responsiveness.

**Planned changes:**
- Apply a full dark mode theme using deep charcoal (#0d0d0d, #1a1a2e) with Electric Blue (#00bfff) and Solar Gold (#ffd700) accents; glassmorphism (backdrop-filter blur, semi-transparent backgrounds, glowing borders) on all cards and panels
- Add a sticky glassmorphic navbar with logo/wordmark, section anchor links (Home, Stats, Products, Calculator, Contact), a "Get a Quote" button, scroll-opacity transition, and a mobile hamburger menu
- Build a full-viewport Hero section with a Three.js/React Three Fiber 3D rotating solar panel or glowing sun model, animated star-field or energy-grid background, a headline, and a "Request Quote" CTA button
- Implement an Interactive Stats section with four animated count-up counters (Energy Saved MWh, CO2 Reduced tons, Homes Powered, Years of Experience) triggered by Intersection Observer on scroll
- Create a Product Showcase grid of at least 4 glassmorphic solar panel cards, each with product image, name, description, key spec, and hover-glow effect in Electric Blue or Solar Gold
- Build a client-side Savings Calculator with sliders for monthly electricity bill and roof size/panel count, displaying real-time estimated monthly savings, annual savings, and CO2 offset
- Add a glassmorphic Contact/Footer section with a Name/Email/Message contact form, placeholder company contact details, social media icons, and a copyright line
- Implement smooth scroll navigation, scroll-triggered fade-in/slide-up entrance animations for all sections, and full responsiveness across 320px, 768px, and 1280px breakpoints

**User-visible outcome:** Users can browse a visually striking single-page solar energy website featuring a 3D animated hero, interactive stats, product showcase, savings calculator, and a contact form — all with a consistent dark glassmorphism aesthetic and smooth animations.
